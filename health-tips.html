🥦 Nutrition Tips

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in iron, calcium, and folic acid.
  • Stay hydrated—drink plenty of water daily.
  • Avoid caffeine, alcohol, and unpasteurized dairy.

🏃‍♀️ Physical Activity

  • Do light exercises like walking or prenatal yoga.
  • Avoid high-risk or contact sports.
  • Consult your doctor before starting anything new.

🧘‍♀️ Mental Wellness

  • Practice deep breathing and relaxation techniques.
  • Get plenty of sleep and rest when needed.
  • Talk to someone if you feel overwhelmed or anxious.
